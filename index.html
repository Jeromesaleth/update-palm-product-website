About Us

Welcome to Malligai Palm Products

Since 2014, Malligai Palm Products has been dedicated to producing high-quality palm products while creating opportunities for women to thrive in the workforce. Founded as a family business, we’ve grown with a strong sense of purpose: to improve lives, create jobs, and bring sustainable products to the market. Our journey started with a vision to offer not just premium palm products, but also meaningful work that empowers women in our community. By providing a platform for women to contribute, learn, and grow, we’ve seen how this opportunity transforms lives, enhances the quality of life, and strengthens the fabric of our business. At Malligai Palm Products, we believe that the success of our business is rooted in the success of our people.We value hard work, innovation, and above all, community. Our commitment to sustainable practices and supporting women workers is at the heart of everything we do

We are proud to have created a work environment where women can build careers, provide for their families, and play an essential role in our growth. The positive impact of these opportunities extends far beyond the workplace, improving the lives of everyone connected to our business.



Thank you for choosing Malligai Palmleaf Products where quality, opportunity, and community come together.
