Palm Products

1. Iddiappa thattu

An Iddiappa thattu is a traditional, round, flat tray made from palm leaves, commonly used in South Indian households for serving food, particularly for dishes like Iddiappam. It is known for its rustic and eco-friendly design, often used in festivals or daily meals.

2. Pen stand

A pen stand made from palm leaves is a natural desk accessory designed to hold pens, pencils, and other writing tools. It combines functionality with a rustic aesthetic, offering a sustainable and traditional alternative to plastic or metal pen stands.
