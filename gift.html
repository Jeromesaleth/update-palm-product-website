Palm Products

1. Gift box

A gift box is a decorative container used to present gifts.

2. jewel small box

A small jewelry box is a compact container designed to store and organize jewelry such as rings, earrings, necklaces, and bracelets.

3. jewel medium box

A medium jewelry box is a slightly larger container designed to store and organize various pieces of jewelry, such as rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets.

4. jewel box

A jewel box is a container designed to store and organize jewelry. It often features compartments or trays to keep items like rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets safe, organized, and easily accessible. Jewel boxes are both functional and decorative, adding elegance to any space.

5. Colorful jewelry box

A colorful jewelry box is a vibrant, decorative container designed to organize and protect jewelry, adding a playful and stylish touch to any space.
